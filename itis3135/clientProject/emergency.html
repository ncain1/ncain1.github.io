Emergency Protocol!

What do we classify as an emergency event?

An emergency event is any event which poses an immediate threat or danger upon your life or others. This page is designed to inform you on local Protocols when faced with one of these events!

IN THE EVENT OF A LIFE THREATENING SITUATION PLEASE CALL 911!

Tornado Protocol

This graphic details the correct places within the home to hunker down in the event of a tornado in the area. Stay away from windows and gather near the center of the house preferrably on a ground floor to increase your and your family's overall safety.

Tornado Warnings and Watches

No matter if it's a watch, warning, or outlook you should keep an eye on weather forecasts and local news in order to prepare accordingly and keep your family safe to the best of your ability!

Hurricane Protocol

With North Carolina being a costal state on the Atlantic Ocean you can never rule out the possibility of a hurricane. Especially in recent memory. The above graphic will help you to prepare at every stage of the hurricane process.

Heat Exhaustion

With Summer right around the corner in North Carolina we are no stranger to devastating heat waves. Exercise extreme caution when spending extended periods of times outside in the sun as it could be detrimental to your health. The above graphic outlines warning signs that you may have taken in a little too much sun. Stay cool, stay hydated, and have fun!
