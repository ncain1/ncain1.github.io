Rock Collecting

What Is It?

Rock collecting, also known as rockhounding, is a fascinating hobby that appeals to individuals of all ages and backgrounds. Many rockhounders prospect for the thrill of exploration of rare and common rocks, gems, and minerals.

The popular American version of rockhounding that we have come to love and enjoy today first orginated in the 1930's with the incorporation of cars into the hobby which allowed many rockhounds to quickly move from site to site.

About Us!

Our team is founded on a decade of in the field experience prospecting in the mountains of North Carolina and visiting various rockshows in multiple states even as far as Arizona! We hold and unwavering love for the natural beauty produced within the Earth's crust which takes millions of years to manifest.

Over our time in the hobby we have managed to obtain many rare minerals and beautiful specimens from across the country. Our personal favorite that we have got our hands on is the mineral fulgurite which is natural tubes or crusts of glass formed by the fusion of silica (quartz) sand or rock from a lightning strike.
